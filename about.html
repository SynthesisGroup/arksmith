Our Origins

A Story of Purposeful Existence

Arksmith is a furniture manufacturing company that takes inspiration from the scriptural narrative of the Ark, a vessel built to withstand the cataclysmic flood and sustain life for a world made new. At its heart is the passion to build great and lasting things for spaces with great purposes—things made with the home-grown heart of a company built from the backyard up, the rich soul of a proud Pampango tradition, and all the boldness and sleekness the modern world offers.

Arksmith traces its heritage back to the Post-Pinatubo Era in Pampanga. The fiery cataclysm reshaped life in the province and in the entire Central Luzon region. The manufacturing sector sustained heavy damage, and the furniture industry was among the hardest hit.

In its aftermath, established manufacturers moved to nearby provinces like Tarlac, teaching the craft as they practiced it with the uninitiated, in effect giving birth to cottage or backyard furniture businesses. The craft sustained entire communities where furniture subcontracting soon became the primary source of income. It shaped lives as young children learned the craft as an integral part of Kapampangan childhood, where it served as a rite of passage. The craft grew and thrived again—but not for long.

The second-largest volcanic eruption of the 20 th century could not extinguish Pampanga’s furniture craft and industry, but the realities of a global economy in the 21 st century just might. As demand for mass-produced items grew, big firms started to commercialize and shift production to cheaper locales like China. Times were changing. Furniture craftsmanship, culture, and tradition were on the decline. This is the to shape and fashion along their vision.

Ruwayna found her calling in reviving what had been a dying craft. And yet for her it was more than just that; it was a discipline, an entire tradition, on its own. It is her belief that progress is not a zero-sum game: the modern and the traditional were not binary, mutually exclusive forces. Instead they could be wedded together by integrating tried-and-tested disciplines with modern techniques in ways that do not compromise quality and that can fill the demands of the modern global market.

To execute this vision, and to better showcase the richness of Kapampangan furniture, she took up Interior Design at the University of the Philippines Diliman and later pursued Furniture Design at the School of Fashion and the Arts.

Glenn, being the seasoned entrepreneur in the husband-wife tandem, employed the principle of Lean Operations in managing Arksmith from the very beginning. Having witnessed how furniture companies closed down due to unforeseen circumstances, the del Rosarios strive to provide greater customer satisfaction while using as few resources as possible. Today, Arksmith flourishes amidst the pandemic and impending global economic crisis largely because of the company’s core value of purposeful existence.

Why Choose Us?

Advantages

Choose synergy between old and new.

Arksmith guarantees superior products through the synergy it fosters between seasoned craftsmen shaped by decades of tradition and professionals armed with the best education in design and manufacturing.

Choose attention to detail.

Different projects will have different needs, but everything boils down to attention to detail. With nearly a decade of experience in dealing with projects both small and large in scale, Arksmith has created its very own system, ensuring a stress-free process for clients - from the selection of furniture pieces down to delivery and maintenance.

Choose satisfaction.

Arksmith’s roster of satisfied clients ranging from private homeowners to large corporations is proof enough of its excellent service and quality.

Choose value for money.

Furniture pieces are investments on their own. Pieces from neighboring markets might look cheaper at face value, but they are notorious for their short lifespans—whatever you save initially will be wiped out when you have to buy a replacement in a year’s time. You also have to factor in long lead times and exorbitant freight charges and taxes when importing. Most importantly, Arksmith offers a service warranty, and you can easily reach the company to retouch and refurbish your furniture after a few years. Companies from these other markets do not give the same guarantee, whether pieces are damaged in transit or due to normal wear and tear.

Choose the confidence of proximity.

Because of its strategic location, you can easily visit the Arksmith shop to inspect the materials it uses and the methods it employs. This also means minimal to no damage during delivery, unlike when shipping imported pieces. You can also be confident that all materials used are well-suited to the local weather and climate.

Choose speed and capability.

Arksmith has not yet gone the full automation route like factories in other markets—but it doesn’t have to. It keeps up with the capacity requirements of its clients by employing multiple craft-specific shops and sophisticated construction strategies that speed up production time.
